ğŸš‘ SALVAGE METHOD #1: MEDEVAC RESCUE CREW RECOVERY

CONCEPT: Rescue stranded vehicle crews and deliver them to MASH zones

How It Works

  1. When friendly vehicles are destroyed, there's a % chance the crew survives
    (Default: 50% survival chance, configurable per coalition)
  2. After a 5-minute delay (battle clearance), surviving crew spawns near the wreck with a MEDEVAC mission announcement
  3. Crew will:
    • Pop smoke when rescue helicopter approaches (8km detection)
    • Send colorful radio messages ("Follow the smoke!")
    • Wait up to 1 hour before being declared KIA
    • May include MANPADS soldier for self-defense (10% chance)
  4. Load crew via normal troop pickup (land near crew, auto-loads)
  5. Fly to any MASH (Mobile Army Surgical Hospital) zone
  6. Land in MASH zone and wait 15 seconds for automatic crew offload
  7. Salvage points awarded based on vehicle value!

Salvage Value Scale

Value is determined by the destroyed vehicle type (from catalog):

Vehicle Type Salvage Points Examples
Light Vehicles 1-5 points Humvees, trucks, light armor
Medium Vehicles 5-15 points APCs, IFVs, light tanks
Heavy Vehicles 15-30 points MBTs, heavy armor
Special Assets 30-50 points SAMs, artillery, C2 vehicles
Example: Rescue a T-90 crew = ~25 salvage points

MEDEVAC F10 Menu Commands

  • F10 > Operations > MEDEVAC > List Active Missions - Shows all pending rescues with locations
  • F10 > Operations > MEDEVAC > Vectors to Nearest Crew - Bearing/distance to closest MEDEVAC
  • F10 > Operations > MEDEVAC > Coalition Salvage Points - Check your coalition's total salvage balance

ğŸ“¦ SALVAGE METHOD #2: SLING-LOAD RECOVERY EQUIPMENT SALVAGE

CONCEPT: Recover enemy equipment wreckage via DCS sling-load mechanics

How It Works

  1. When ENEMY ground units die, there's a 15% chance (configurable) to spawn a physical cargo crate near the wreck for YOUR coalition to collect
  2. Crate naming:
    • SALVAGE-B-XXXXXX (Blue coalition can collect, spawns from RED deaths)
    • SALVAGE-R-XXXXXX (Red coalition can collect, spawns from BLUE deaths)
  3. Orange smoke marks crate location for 2 minutes after spawn
  4. Crate has a random weight class (determines helicopter requirement & reward)
  5. Use standard DCS F6 RADIO MENU sling-load to hook the crate
  6. Fly to a Salvage Collection Zone (create via F10 > Field Tools menu)
  7. Land/drop crate inside the zone boundary
  8. Automatic detection awards salvage points based on weight + condition!
  9. Crate expires after 3 HOURS if not collected (warnings at 30min & 5min)
NOT hover-pickup! This is pure DCS sling-load mechanics using F6 RADIO MENU!

Spawn Restrictions

Salvage crates will NOT spawn:

  • Within 1000m of active pickup zones (prevents clutter)
  • Within 1km of airbases (avoids spawn on runways)
  • 10-25 meters from wreck location (random placement)

Lifecycle & Warnings

  • Spawn: Orange smoke + coalition announcement (grid, weight, estimated value)
  • 30 Minutes Remaining: First warning message
  • 5 Minutes Remaining: Urgent warning message
  • Expiration: Crate removed + expiration message
  • Total Lifetime: 3 HOURS (10,800 seconds, configurable)

ğŸ’° Using Salvage Points

What Are Salvage Points?

Salvage points are a coalition-wide resource pool that allows you to build equipment that is normally out-of-stock at your current location.

When Salvage Is Used

Salvage auto-applies when:

  1. You request a crate that is OUT OF STOCK at nearest supply zone
  2. Coalition has enough salvage points to cover the cost
  3. Cost = item's required crate count (e.g., M1 Abrams = 3 crates = 3 salvage)

Salvage Balance

Check your coalition's salvage point balance:

F10 > Operations > MEDEVAC > Coalition Salvage Points

Or build/request menu will show salvage balance when out-of-stock

ğŸ”§ Troubleshooting

"I can't sling-load the salvage crate!"

  • Use DCS F6 RADIO MENU, not F10 CTLD hover pickup
  • Make sure helicopter supports sling-load (Huey, Hip, Chinook, etc.)
  • Check crate weight vs. helicopter capacity

"Crate disappeared before I got there!"

  • Crates expire after 3 hours
  • Check F10 > Navigation > Vectors for time remaining
  • Warnings sent at 30min and 5min

"I didn't get full reward for my delivery!"

  • Check crate health - damage reduces reward by up to 50%
  • Fly smoothly, avoid crashes, gentle landings
  • Undamaged crates give 50% BONUS!

"No MEDEVAC missions spawning!"

  • Check crew survival chance settings (default 50%)
  • Only friendly vehicle deaths spawn MEDEVAC
  • 5-minute delay after death before crew spawns

"Where do I create Salvage Collection Zones?"

  • F10 > Field Tools > Salvage Collection Zones > Create Salvage Zone Here
  • Zone spawns at your current position with 300m radius
